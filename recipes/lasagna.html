Lasagna

Description

This recipe is for a classic lasagna dish. The ingredients are as follows:

  • 2 tbsp of olive oil
  • 750g of lean beef mince
  • 90g pack prosciutto
  • 800g passata
  • 200ml of hot beef stock
  • Nutmeg
  • 300g of fresh lasagna sheets
  • white sauce
  • 125g ball mozarella (Torn into thin strips)
